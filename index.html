About VoteNova

Why elections deserve better infrastructure

VoteNova was built because existing voting platforms were either outdated, opaque, or too expensive for small organizations. We believe every group — from a 20-person team to a 20,000-person city district — deserves the same quality.

What we stand for

Built on three promises

🔒

Unbreakable security

Every vote is cryptographically signed and stored immutably. Audit logs are available for every election at any time.

👁️

Full transparency

Voters can verify their vote was counted — without revealing who they voted for. Results are published the moment polls close.

⚡

Effortless to run

Admins can set up a full election — candidates, schedule, eligible voters — in under 10 minutes. No technical knowledge required.
